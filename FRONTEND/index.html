Library Management
System

The Library Management System serves as the centralized digital backbone for the Sir Syed University knowledge repository. Designed for high-concurrency environments, this engine orchestrates the entire lifecycle of academic resources, from automated cataloging and real-time inventory tracking to faculty-led circulation audits.

By replacing legacy manual processes, this system ensures institutional efficiency, secure authentication, and seamless access to critical research assets for the entire SSUET engineering and technology community.

Our commitment is to provide a robust digital infrastructure that supports both students and faculty in their pursuit of research.

Detailed Circulation Rules

  • Maximum limit: 3 physical books per student.
  • Standard loan duration: 14 days; renewals based on availability.
  • Late fines: PKR 100 per day per book.
